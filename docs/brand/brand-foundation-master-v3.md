# **DOCUMENT 05  ·  CORE POSITIONING STATEMENT (REVISED MAY 2026)**

***This document is the intellectual architecture on which every page of the website is built. It answers the question: why does this practice exist, who is it for, and how is it categorically different from both conventional medicine and the alternative-care economy?***
***The current positioning supersedes the prior versions in v1.0 and v2.0, and is consistent with North Star Vision v4.0 Section 07.***

## **THE POSITIONING STATEMENT**

***Tagge Precision Health is concierge precision medicine for people who have been let down by the care they have been getting.***
***Medicine has always existed to treat disease. Conventional, functional, and integrative medicine all come from the same ethos. They differ in tools and aesthetic. They do not differ in foundation. None of them has a definition of health, and none of them has a standard of care for delivering it.***
***This practice is built differently. Built on a definition of health. Built to deliver a standard of care for health. Built to optimize the patient's health through measure, compare, balance — the methodology of Health Optimization Medicine.***
***The patient enters one of two ways: by exploring their first month ($500, fully refundable), or by scheduling a complimentary call. Both paths lead to the same product: a continuous physician relationship grounded in the patient's data, governed by the Intervention Accountability Standard, and oriented toward the long arc of their health.***
***Every recommendation traces to a specific finding. Every finding acts through a known mechanism. Every intervention earns its place. There are no protocols, no supplement bundles, no interventions without a reason. There is one Plan, written for one patient, refined as their biology evolves.***
***This is what concierge precision medicine looks like when it is held to the standard medicine itself has not yet defined.***

## **THE DIFFERENTIATION ARCHITECTURE**

***Four legs:***

### **Leg 1  ·  Physician-Led**

***Dr. Tagge's conventional medical training and board eligibility in family medicine creates a credentialing moat that cannot be replicated by non-physician competitors.***

### **Leg 2  ·  Salutogenic Foundation**

***The practice operates from a definition of health rather than from a definition of disease. This is philosophically and methodologically distinct from any other clinical practice the patient has access to. This is the load-bearing differentiation.***

### **Leg 3  ·  Metabolomics as Diagnostic Foundation**

***Metabolomics is the primary diagnostic lens. This generates data competitors do not have, interpreted against optimal ranges rather than disease cutoffs.***

### **Leg 4  ·  The Intervention Accountability Standard**

***Every intervention is traceable to a finding, acts through a mechanism, carries an evidence tier, and is measured against the baseline that justified it. This is the methodological floor underneath the salutogenic foundation.***

## **THE ONE-LINE COMPETITIVE SUMMARY**

***We are the practice built on a definition of health and the methodology to deliver it — where every recommendation traces to your data, accountable to evidence, and measured against outcomes.***

## **THE COMPETITIVE QUESTION**

***The question that separates this practice from every other model in the market — the question no competitor can answer with the same specificity — is not “Do you offer metabolomics?” or “Do you offer peptide therapy?”***
***The question is:***
***Do you have a definition of health, and a standard of care for delivering it?***
***This practice does. None of the others do.***
***This is what we are building.***

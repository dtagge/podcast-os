# **01  THE VISION**

***What we are building***
***Tagge Precision Health is building something that does not yet exist in medicine: a standard of care for health.***
***Medicine has always existed to treat disease. Conventional, functional, and integrative medicine all share this orientation. They differ in tools, vocabulary, and aesthetic. They do not differ in foundation. None of them has a definition of health, and none of them has a standard of care for delivering it.***
***This practice is built differently. Built on a definition of what health actually is. Built to deliver a standard of care that holds itself to that definition. Built to optimize each patient's health through the methodology of Health Optimization Medicine: measure, compare, balance. Detection and correction at the systems level. Network-wide rebalancing rather than single-target treatment.***
***This is salutogenic medical practice. A category that has not existed at scale before now.***

In a single session, this model produced: a 14-priority metabolomic analysis with full mechanistic rationale and evidence tier classification for each finding; a patient-facing optimization report with actionable supplement protocol, lifestyle prescription, and phased timeline; a clinical language standards document; product selection guidance; patient communication drafts; and a Fullscript protocol with precise instructions. # **02  THE INTERVENTION ACCOUNTABILITY STANDARD**

***The methodological floor***
***Every intervention this practice prescribes — every peptide, every supplement, every hormone, every lifestyle modification, every dietary recommendation — is traceable to a specific finding in the patient's data, acts through a defined mechanism, and carries an evidence tier the patient will know before they begin.***
***This is not a quality aspiration. It is the methodological floor. It is what separates this practice from every other model in the market — not because other practices don't try to be good, but because no other practice has built the diagnostic foundation, the clinical framework, and the synthesis infrastructure required to actually deliver this standard at scale.***
***The standard applies universally across intervention classes: peptide therapy, supplements, hormone therapy, lifestyle prescriptions, dietary modifications, and any future therapeutic modality. There is no intervention class that is exempt.***

# **03  WHAT THIS IS NOT**

***The boundaries matter***
- ***AI replacing clinical judgment. The AI holds context and generates synthesis. The physician makes every clinical decision, interprets every finding, and maintains the patient relationship. The model requires an exceptional physician to function — it amplifies expertise, it does not substitute for it.***
- ***Functional medicine with technology. Functional medicine uses similar tools but lacks the mechanistic rigor, optimal reference ranges, and systems-biology framework of HOMe/HOPe. This is a distinct clinical methodology — detection and correction, not diagnosis and treatment.***
- ***A supplement dispensary. The protocol emerges from data, not from general wellness recommendations. The Intervention Accountability Standard governs every recommendation. The physician is the product — not the supplements, not the peptides, not the hormones.***
- ***A peptide clinic. Peptide therapy is an intervention class within the framework, not a service line. It enters the protocol only when metabolomic, immunological, or mitochondrial findings justify it.***
- ***A diagnostic algorithm. Pattern recognition is a component, but the model is Bayesian, not deterministic. Multiple converging findings shift probability — they do not produce automatic outputs. Clinical judgment mediates every interpretation.***
- ***Concierge medicine rebranded. Concierge medicine offers better access to standard care. This model offers a categorically different quality of analysis — one that conventional medicine, at any price point, currently cannot match because it requires both the HOMe/HOPe framework and AI synthesis bandwidth simultaneously.***
- ***Scalable without the physician. The physician's interpretive layer is irreplaceable. What scales is the synthesis and documentation bandwidth — the 4-hour work that can become 45 minutes. The clinical judgment, the patient relationship, and the accountability for the protocol remain with Dr. Tagge.***

# **05  THE COMPETITIVE MOAT**

***Why this is difficult to replicate***
- ***The Salutogenic Foundation. Most medicine does not have a definition of health. This practice does, and operates from it. That foundation is not a marketing claim — it is a clinical methodology grounded in the HOMe/HOPe framework. Replicating the foundation requires both the framework training and the conviction to operate from it.***
- ***The Intervention Accountability Standard. The universal standard that governs every recommendation this practice makes. No intervention — peptide, supplement, hormone, lifestyle, dietary — enters the protocol without a traceable finding, a defined mechanism, and a communicated evidence tier.***
- ***The HOMe/HOPe Framework. The 7-pillar clinical methodology developed by Dr. Ted Achacoso is not widely practiced. Most functional medicine practitioners lack the systems-biology depth to apply it rigorously. It provides the interpretive lens that makes metabolomic data clinically actionable rather than just informative.***
- ***The Optimal Reference Standard. Comparing biomarkers against 21–30-year-old physiology rather than disease-based ranges requires both the methodological conviction and the clinical knowledge to interpret findings outside conventional cut-offs. This produces genuinely different clinical conclusions from the same data.***
- ***The Accumulated Knowledge Base. Every clinical case, pillar document, language standard, and protocol decision captured in the project knowledge base compounds. Each session makes the AI synthesis better calibrated to the practice's specific clinical standards.***
- ***The Physician's Expertise. The model requires a physician who has internalized HOMe/HOPe methodology, has deep biochemistry knowledge, and has the clinical confidence to make precision interventions based on functional rather than structural findings. This combination is rare.***
- ***The AI-Enhanced Synthesis Layer. The AI synthesis holds the entire patient picture simultaneously — clinical history, metabolomics, genetics, supplements, wearable data, brand voice, evidence tiers. No EHR, no specialist, and no conventional clinical workflow does this.***
- ***Proof of Clinical Outcome. The April 2026 case produced a mechanistically coherent explanation for a 9-year undiagnosed condition, a therapeutic protocol, and a patient communication — all in a single session. This is a demonstrable clinical outcome that serves as the foundation for practice credibility.***
- ***Evidence Tier Transparency. Every intervention carries a communicated evidence tier: strong clinical (RCTs, regulatory approval), moderate clinical (phase II or established use data), strong preclinical (consistent animal data), or early/emerging.***
